Quick answers.

Is TradeZona really free?
Yes. The Free plan is free forever — 1 journal, basic analytics, unlimited trades, no credit card required. The Pro plan is $5/month and unlocks unlimited journals, full analytics, screenshot gallery, export/import, and more. Cancel anytime.
Where is my data stored?
Your trades are stored securely in our cloud database powered by Supabase. All data is protected with row-level security — only you can access your journals and trades. We never sell your data or use it for advertising.
Can I use it on multiple devices?
Yes — simply sign in on any device and your trades, journals, and settings are all there instantly. No manual export or sync needed. This works on Free and Pro plans.
Do I need to create an account?
Yes — TradeZona requires a free account (email + password) to securely store and sync your trade data across devices. Sign up takes about 15 seconds with no credit card required.
Can I track multiple trading accounts?
Pro users can create unlimited journals — one for Forex, one for Crypto, one for your prop firm account. Free users get 1 journal. Each journal has its own capital, PIN, tags, and trade history completely separate.
How does billing work for Pro?
Pro is $5/month, billed monthly. Cancel at any time with no long-term commitment. If you cancel, you keep your data and revert to the Free plan limits. Your trades are never deleted.
Can I export my data?
Yes — Pro users can export a full backup of their journal (trades + all settings) as a .json file, or trades-only as a simpler export. You can also import a backup to restore or migrate journals between accounts.

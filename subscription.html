Common questions
Is the free plan really free forever?
Yes. The free plan is free forever with no credit card required. You get 1 journal, unlimited trade logs, basic analytics, and limited calendar — no strings attached.
What's the difference between Monthly and Annual?
Monthly is $15/month billed each month. Annual is $150/year — that's $12.50/month, saving you $30 compared to monthly. Both include the exact same Pro features.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from Manage Billing. You keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period, then revert to Free. Your trades and data are never deleted.
What happens to extra journals if I cancel?
Extra journals become read-only after downgrade. All data is preserved — you just can't add new trades until you re-upgrade. Your primary journal works normally.
How does the referral program work?
Share your referral link from your Profile page. When someone signs up through your link and subscribes to Pro, you get 30 free days of Pro added to your account automatically.
Where is my data stored?
Securely in Supabase with row-level security — only you can access your data. Pro users can export everything as a .json backup (including images) or .csv for spreadsheet analysis.

Quick answers

How does billing work?
Pro is $5/month, billed monthly via Stripe. Cancel at any time — no long-term commitment. If you cancel, you keep your data and revert to Free plan limits. Your trades are never deleted.
What happens to my data if I downgrade?
All your trades, journals, and images are preserved. You'll just lose access to Pro features like multiple journals, the calculator, and full analytics. Nothing is deleted.
Is the checkout secure?
Yes. Payments are handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your card details. TradeZona only receives a confirmation that your subscription is active.
Can I get a refund?
Contact us within 7 days of your charge and we'll refund you, no questions asked. After 7 days, charges are final but you can cancel to avoid future billing.

Questions & Answers
Is the free plan really free forever?
Yes. The free plan is free forever with no credit card required. You get 1 journal, unlimited trades, and basic analytics.
Can I cancel my Pro plan anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime with no long-term commitment. If you cancel, you keep your data and revert to the Free plan limits.
Where is my data stored?
All your data is stored securely in our database powered by Supabase, protected by row-level security.
What happens to my data if I downgrade?
Your data is always safe. If you downgrade, you'll be limited to 1 journal but all existing data remains intact.

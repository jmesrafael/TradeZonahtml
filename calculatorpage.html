Risk 1–2% per trade

Professional traders rarely risk more than 1–2% of their account on a single trade. This keeps drawdowns manageable and lets you survive losing streaks.

Aim for 2R or better

A minimum 2:1 reward-to-risk ratio means you only need a 34% win rate to break even. At 50% win rate with 2R, you grow consistently.

Track every trade

Knowing your position size is only half the edge. The other half is logging every trade so you can see which setups actually work for you.
